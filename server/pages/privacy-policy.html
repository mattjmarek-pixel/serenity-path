Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Serenity Path ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Your name (optional, for personalized greetings)
  • Sobriety date (stored locally on your device)
  • Journal entries (stored locally on your device)
  • Personal mantra and preferences (stored locally on your device)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage analytics (anonymous, aggregated data only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's functionality
  • To personalize your experience within the app
  • To process voluntary support contributions (via secure payment processors)

Data Storage and Security

Most of your personal data (sobriety date, journal entries, preferences) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information that is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Stripe) - subject to their privacy policy
  • App analytics - only anonymous, aggregated data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email.
