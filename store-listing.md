## Full Description
Serenity Path is a calm, private companion for your AA and NA recovery journey.

Whether you're on day one or celebrating years of sobriety, Serenity Path gives you the tools to stay connected to your program — anytime, anywhere.

FEATURES:
- Sobriety counter — track your days, hours, and minutes with milestone celebrations
- Daily reflections — 366 meditations, one for every day of the year
- 12 Steps & Traditions — full reference library with guided reflection questions
- Journal — private, mood-tagged entries stored only on your device
- "I'm Struggling" panic button — one-tap crisis support, guided breathing, and grounding exercises
- Daily check-in — track your mood and spot trends over time
- Gratitude practice — three things daily, with history
- Meeting finder — GPS-based search for nearby AA and NA meetings
- Sobriety chips — traditional milestone chip system through 25 years
- Step work companion — guided questions for all 12 steps
- Push notifications — configurable daily reminders
- 4th Step inventory — private, secure resentment and fear worksheets
- Service work tracker — log and celebrate your Step 12 service

YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS:
All journal entries, step work, and personal data stay on your device. No account required to use the app.

Serenity Path is not affiliated with Alcoholics Anonymous World Services, Inc. or Narcotics Anonymous World Services, Inc.
